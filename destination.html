God’s Own Country! KERALA

Kerala – a divine state in the southern part of India is a home to numerous natural marvels. Embraced by the alluring charm of the silent backwaters, the captivating beauty of the blue lagoons, the charm of the evergreen coconut groves, a bewitching list of tourist destinations, this state is also revered as the “God’s Own Country”!. Whether you are an adventure freak, a leisure seeker, planning for your honeymoon, travelling with family and friends, or yearning for a solo backpacking trip to offbeat destinations, you can always plan a Kerala trip and satiate all your yearnings.
